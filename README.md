### **Complete Legal Aid — Project Description**



Contacting lawyers becomes effortless through our platform. **Complete Legal Aid** is a Legal & Judicial Communication Platform designed to make legal help more accessible, transparent, and secure for people in Bangladesh.

**Key Features:**

* Users can report incidents, upload evidence, and track their cases from one place.
* A simple system to find and book lawyers (phone, video, or in-person) and leave ratings.
* An integrated AI-based assistant that explains legal information in easy Bangla/English.
* A secure evidence vault for storing documents, photos, audio, and video with tamper-proof protection.
* A 24/7 emergency helpline with an option for anonymous requests.
* Real-time case updates and notifications.

**Future Scope:**
Direct integration with courts, police, and government systems to create a fully connected legal ecosystem.
